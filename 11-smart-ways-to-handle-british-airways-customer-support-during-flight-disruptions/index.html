British Airways Customer Service Guide

Main Customer Service Number (US-OTA): 1-800-British Airways or 1-833-769-1476. You can reach a live representative 24/7 for help with booking changes, cancellations, refunds, or compensation queries.

Why Contact a Live Person at British Airways?

  • Flight changes or cancellations: Adjust or cancel your flights, deal with delays.
  • Booking clarification: Get help understanding your reservation details.
  • Refunds and compensation: Complex refund requests and compensation claims.
  • Technical glitches: Resolve booking or payment system issues.

British Airways Contact Options

Calling British Airways Customer Service Hotline

Call 1-833-769-1476 (US-OTA). Listen to the prompts and choose the option that fits your issue. Pressing “0” or saying “agent” can speed up connecting to a real person.

Using British Airways Live Chat Feature

Visit the British Airways website, go to the Help section, and choose the live chat option to speak with a real representative online.

Reaching Out on Social Media

British Airways responds quickly via social platforms like Twitter and Facebook for general inquiries.

Utilizing the British Airways Mobile App

Use the app to contact customer service via call or chat directly from your mobile device.

Emailing British Airways Support

Email is suitable for less urgent issues and offers written documentation. Expect responses within 24-48 hours.

Step-by-Step: Talking to a Live Person via Phone

  1. Listen to the automated prompts.
  2. Select the option that matches your issue (e.g., “existing reservations”).
  3. Press “0” or repeat “agent” if needed to bypass prompts.
